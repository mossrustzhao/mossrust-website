$ cat ABOUT.txt

Founded in 1993 as a bamboo craft workshop, MOSSRUST has been dedicated to artisanal excellence for over three decades. In the early 1990s, we made a decisive pivot to iron — drawn to its durability, its weight, and the way time treats it with such beautiful cruelty.

In 2010, we formally established our import/export trading company, expanding our product line to encompass iron, wood, rattan, and bamboo home goods. Today, our core offering — the hand-forged vintage iron planter — is the culmination of twenty years of process refinement.

$ cat CRAFT.txt

Each planter undergoes over ten manual processes: cutting, welding, grinding, chemical aging, and hand-applied finishing. We specialize in the copper-green verdigris patina — achieved through proprietary chemical treatment and meticulous hand-brushing techniques. The result: layered depth, a coarse-yet-warm tactile surface, and a finish that suggests an artifact rescued from an old estate garden rather than something newly made.

No two pieces age identically. That is the point.

$ cat REACH.txt

MOSSRUST products ship to Europe, North America, and Australia. Our planters and home accents are found in gardens, patio spaces, balconies, commercial interiors, and styled living rooms — anywhere the language of rust, patina, and hand-work speaks louder than the hum of a factory line.
